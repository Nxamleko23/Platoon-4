Conduct of Platoon 4

Platoon 4 is dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of conduct. Our core values include discipline, unity, and mutual respect among all members. Every member is expected to act with professionalism and responsibility everyday.

Ill-Disciplined

  • Disrespecting The leader: Failing to follow orders or showing disregard for leadership instructions.
  • Tardiness: Arriving late for drills, and parade.
  • Improper Dress Code: Wearing of uniform problems during official duties.
  • Negligence of Duty: Not completing assigned tasks or roles effectively.
  • Disruptive Behavior: Causing unnecessary noise, distraction, or disturbances during parade.
  • Lack of Team Spirit: Refusing to cooperate or work in harmony with fellow platoon members.
